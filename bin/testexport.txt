Banana Bread, serves 12

prep time: 20, cook time: 35, total time: 55

Ingredients: 
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup mashed ripe bananas (about 3)
3/4 cup buttermilk or sour milk*
1/2 cup shortening
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs

Instructions: 
Preheat oven to 350F
Grease and lightly flour two loaf baking pans and set aside 
Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt 
Add bananas, buttermilk or sour milk, shortening, and vanilla
Beat with an electric mixer on low speed until combined 
Add eggs; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes 
Pour batter into the prepared pans
Bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

2 cups buttermilk
1/4 cup melted unsalted butter, plus some for frying

Instructions:

Chicken Alfredo, serves 4

prep time: 10, cook time: 15, total time: 25

Ingredients: 
Kosher salt
12 oz fettucine
Olive oil, for tossing
12 oz boneless, skinless chicken breast (about 2)
Freshly ground black epepper
1 stick (8 tbsp) unsalted butter
2 cups heavy cream
2 pinches freshly grated nutmeg
1.5 cups freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Instructions: 
Bring large pot of water to a boil, salting generously
Add pasta and boil according to package directions until al dente, tender but still slightly firm.
Strain and toss with a splash of olive oil
Slice chicken into 1/4 inch strips, laying them on a plate
Season with salt and pepper
Heat a large skillet over medium heat, adding 2 tbsp butter
After butter melts, raise heat to medium high and add chicken
Cook without moving until underside has browned, 1-2 minutes
Flip chicken and cook until other side has browned and cooked, 2-3 minutes
Transfer chicken to a bowl
Reduce heat to medium and add 6 tbsp butter
Deglaze the pan with the butter, scraping the bottom of the skillet with a wooden spoon to release the browned bits
When butter is mostly melted, whisk in cream and nutmeg and simmer, then cook for 2 minutes
Lower heat to warm
Whisk Parmigiano-Reggiano into sauce
Add chicken and pasta, season with salt and pepper

Fajitas, serves 8

prep time: 10, cook time: 10, total time: 20

Ingredients: 
3/4 lb top sirloin steak
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 garlic clove, finely minced
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
8 flour tortillas
1 -2 onion
2 small peppers

Instructions: 
Slice steak into thin strips
In bowl, mix together 1 tablespoons olive oil, lime juice, garlic, chili powder, cumin, hot pepper flakes, black pepper & salt
Add beef strips and stir to coat, set aside.
Wrap tortillas in foil and place in 350? oven for 5-10 minutes or until heated through.
Cut onions in half lengthwise and slice into strips, cut your peppers into strips.
In large non stick skillet over medium high heat, heat remaining tablespoons of olive oil.
Add onions & peppers stirring for 3-4 minutes, until softened; transfer to a bowl and set aside.
Add beef to skillet, cook, stirring for 3-4 minutes or until they lose their red color.
Return onions and peppers to skillet; stir for about one minute.
To serve, spoon a portion of the beef mixture down the centre of each tortilla, top with your desired toppings , fold bottom of tortilla up over filling, fold the sides in, overlapping.

Steak and Arugula Salad, serves 4

prep time: 0, cook time: 20, total time: 20

Ingredients: 
1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
4 (4-ounce) flat-iron steaks
2 lemons, halved
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
4 cups loosely packed baby arugula
1/4 cup (1 ounce) shaved fresh Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Instructions: 
Prepare pan on medium high heat
Rub thyme, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper on steak
Cook steak, remove from pan
Cook lemons for 3 minutes after placing cut side down into pan
Cut steaks across grain
Combine remaining pepper, chives, extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, dijon mustard, and 1/8 tsp salt
Drizzle over arugula and toss
Arrange steak, arugula, and lemon half on each plate and top with cheese
